After causing her sister's death in a zoo accident, Maya overdoses in her mother's attic. On a far-away campus, a lonely cafeteria employee named Claudio is shot by ruthless scientists. Meeting in the afterlife, the unlikely duo is welcomed by Finn, an angel who pushes people towards life-changing insights, hoping to wake them from their near-death experiences. Unfortunately, Finn has a very poor understanding of human psychology, solely relying on academic publications to design his reanimation program. As Maya and Claudio's brains run out of oxygen, their afterlife experiences become ever-stranger and a return to life slips out of reach.
